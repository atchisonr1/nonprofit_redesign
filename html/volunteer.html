Volunteer Opportunities

Pet Assistance, Inc. is a big organization. We are big because we are in the important business of helping people and their pets, and we help save the lives of countless animals whose owners cannot afford emergency care or surgery for life threatening injuries or ailments. Yet we are very small in terms of our assets and overhead.

People often call to volunteer. We do not have a shelter, therefore we do not need help with cleaning cages or walking dogs.

Yet, we do need volunteers to handle the numerous calls for help that come in from all over the country. We need volunteers with some professional or serious 'life experience' in working with animals. We need people who can determine if the call is about a real medical emergency that needs immediate attention, or just about elective surgery or an ongoing manageable problem. They must be familiar with all sorts of problems or be able to get the proper information quickly.

We will train telephone representatives. Please let us know if you are interested.


Other ways to volunteer:

  • Fundraising
  • Collection cans
  • Foster a pet
  • Public relations
  • Press releases
  • Manage our website
  • Facebook
  • Other ideas are welcome!

Please reach out to us via our online form or email us at petassistance1973@gmail.com.
